Save a homeless cat or dog with art... all it takes is a little heart!

Be a Part of the Give a Heart Campaign. When you give the gift of a Heart Art Kit all proceeds will go to Beautiful Together Animal Sanctuary, a non-profit dedicated to rescuing homeless dogs and cats from overcrowded shelters.

Order a Heart Art Kit

How to Give a Heart

It's Easy as 1 ~ 2 ~3

  1. Make a $100 donation and receive your Heart Art Kit which includes a 6"X6" canvas and paint set.
  2. Paint a heart on your canvas any way you choose or gift the kit to someone else to paint
  3. Return the painted canvas to Beautiful Together
Each heart will become part of a one-of-kind collection of 100 hand painted hearts!

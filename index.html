When you give the gift of a Heart Art Kit all proceeds will go to Beautiful Together Animal Sanctuary, a non-profit dedicated to rescuing homeless dogs and cats from overcrowded shelters.

Order a Heart Art Kit

Getting Involved

For a $100 donation, you will receive a Heart Art Kit to give as a gift to anyone of your choice…even yourself! The recipient of the gift will be asked to paint a heart and mail it back or drop it off to a given location and it will become part of a beautiful collection of 100 hand painted hearts.

Order a Heart Art Kit

Buy A Kit

Equipped with a 6” X 6” canvas and a complete color and brush set, the recipient of the kit has full creative freedom to paint whatever heart they please.

Be A Sponsor

You can simply donate! Your donation will go to the Heart Art Kit Fund for a person who wants to paint but may not have the means to contribute.

Have Fun

You don’t need to be an artist to participate! This is not a contest. Just have fun and paint a heart with all your heart!
